Goa


It is located about 250 miles (400 km) south of Mumbai (Bombay). One of India's smallest states, it is bounded by the states of Maharashtra on the north and Karnataka on the east and south and by the Arabian Sea on the west. The capital is Panaji (Panjim), on the north-central coast of the mainland district.
